FITB constraints to design around

  • Spend-by-geo & campaign-level data not yet fully integrated — tolerate manual/batch pulls at first.
  • FITB reviews budget monthly/quarterly today — Velocity Lens is deliberately ahead of their roadmap.
  • ~5.5-month demo→build-approval lead time (Velocity Lens precedent) — a credible read-only MVP de-risks approval.
  • Validate on real RV data (TPG, Bankrate, Frontier, Energy via Mason) before FITB deployment.
  • FITB's own AI is early (Energy = Slack chatbot; AI-coding adoption ~18 months out).

Phased roadmap (~30 weeks)

0

Foundations · wk 1–6

RBAC, connectors (Google/MS/Meta + Snowflake) w/ manual-pull fallback, Snowflake + dbt + semantic layer (CPIHH, funded-accts-per-media-$), app shell. Validate on RV data. Exit: governed acquisition KPIs on a dashboard.

1

Read-only Velocity Lens MVP · wk 7–14 — first shippable / FITB demo

Opportunity scan, optimization curves, top-mover curves, geo reallocation simulation, SoV, read-only dashboards + analysis chat + deliverables. No live writes. Ref: Graphed + Parallel.

2

Compliant creative · wk 15–22

Creative-performance-hypothesis workflow, performance-driven generation with compliance guardrails + required human sign-off. Ref: Atria, constrained for a regulated advertiser.

3

Agentic deployment + control · wk 23–30 — gated by read-only-first decision

Policy & approval, API deploy with mandatory human confirmation, rollback, sync-back, recommendations, monitoring, MCP server. Ref: GlitchAds under Parallel governance.

4

Hardening & forward bets

Enterprise security/SOC 2 posture, audit-log export, agentic-search readiness. Accounts for FITB's ~5.5-mo approval and ~18-mo AI-coding horizon.

Top risks

  • FITB data not integrated — build read-only on what lands; manual/batch fallback; don't block MVP on full integration.
  • Long approval lead time — read-only MVP is a low-risk thing to approve early; agentic deploy phased behind it.
  • Bank compliance on autonomous spend/creative — mandatory human confirmation, creative guardrails, append-only audit log.
  • Attribution to funded accounts (not ROAS) — model funded-accts-per-media-$ & CPIHH in Snowflake; align with Catherine's reporting foundation.

Reference vendors — what each teaches the Acquisition build

The four public products we studied. They are best-in-class paid-media tools we learn from; Velocity Lens adapts their patterns to a regulated bank advertiser.

Daily optimization loop, budget-to-target-CPA, recommendations, alerts, activation. Informs the Optimization & Activation services (Phase 3).

Graphed data + agents

Connectors → managed pipeline → modeled warehouse → live dashboards + MCP. Informs the data layer and the read-only MVP.

Parallel AI control

Connected-context analysis & human-in-the-loop before any live change + shareable deliverables. The governance model for the whole engine.

Atria creative

Performance-driven creative generation & testing. Informs the compliant-creative phase — constrained with guardrails + sign-off.
